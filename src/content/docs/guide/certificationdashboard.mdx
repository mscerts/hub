## Upcoming Exam Appointment
Here you can see all your scheduled appointments. Currently, you can only have 2 exams scheduled at the same time. You can directly access PearsonVUE portal from here by clicking the **Reschedule** button.
<img src="/upcomingexam.png" alt="Microsoft Certification Dashboard page focused on the Upcoming Exam Appointment section, showing scheduled exam entries and a Reschedule button that links to Pearson VUE"/>
## Certifications
<img src="/dashboardcertifications.png" alt="Certifications section of the Microsoft Certification Dashboard, showing certification cards and options to view, share, and download credentials with expiry details"/>
Here you can access all your certifications, share them around, download them, see when they expire and if you are in the renewal window.
&#x200B;
## Past Exams
<img src="/dashboardexams.png" alt="Past Exams section on the Microsoft Certification Dashboard, listing completed exam records and available actions including View details on provider site"/>
Similarly to the **Certifications** section, you can find all your exams here. One added bonus is that this is another place you can directly access PearsonVUE portal from by choosing the **View details on provider site**.
<img src="/dashboardassessments.png" alt="Past renewal assessments area on the Microsoft Certification Dashboard"/>
Here you can access all your renewal assessment attempts. So, if you fail you can check which areas you were weak in and study up on those more. For more on renewals, see the [Certification Renewal](/guide/certificationrenewal/) guide.
## Microsoft Credential Support
&#x200B;
<img src="/dashboardsupport.png" alt="Microsoft Certification Dashboard support section highlighting the Microsoft certification support link at engagehub.microsoft.com for certification related issues"/>

And lastly, you can see [Microsoft certification support](https://engagehub.microsoft.com/#view/Microsoft_AzureCXP_EngageHub/MsaLanding.ReactView/targetExperience/sfb/sapId/de16815f-2fa5-576e-4946-70cae21a4eeb) URL. This is really important since Microsoft certification support is the **only** place where you can solve certification and profile issues or try to get exams refunded/rescheduled after PearsonVUE messes your exam up and you lose the exam attempt.

Usually contacting PearsonVUE themselves is a huge waste of time. So, this is the best option.

## PearsonVUE Portal

Besides Certification Dashboard, you can also access PearsonVUE Portal using this URL [PearsonVUE Portal](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/certifications/schedule-through-pearson-vue?action=1&WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290)

When you are in PearsonVUE portal, you can navigate to the main page by clicking the **PearsonVUE Home button.**

<img src="/pearsonvueportal.png" alt="Pearson VUE portal dashboard page showing navigation including Pearson VUE Home, My account, and View score reports for accessing past exam score reports, displayed in a web portal interface with a neutral administrative tone"/>

The most important thing here, besides being able to see all upcoming exams and reschedule them, you can access your past exam score reports. The option is located under **My account** \> **View score reports** on the right side of the page.
