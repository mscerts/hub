Best for business users and power users, with a focus on understanding basic concepts or your ability to use an end user tool.

These are the easier exams Microsoft offers. They are not technical, don't require any previous knowledge or experience. All information you need to know is generally in the Microsoft Learn course or it is just common sense.

## Study Time

Plan for 1 to 3 weeks of focused prep, depending on your familiarity with the product and business scenarios.

## Where do I start

### Study Guide
:::tip 
https://aka.ms/examcode-studyguide (for example: https://aka.ms/ab730-studyguide)
:::

The Study Guide is the only document that actually outlines what is on the exam. It is your source of truth for what is on the exam. Always reference it so you stay focused on relevant exam topics and avoid getting sidetracked.
