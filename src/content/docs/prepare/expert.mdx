:::note
Best for experienced cloud professionals and architects, with a focus on technical and architectural depth across services, SKUs, and design decisions.
:::

:::caution
Expert exams are role-based and test practical skills, not just course memorization. They can be very difficult without real hands-on experience with the products.
:::

## Study Time

Average study time for expert exams is 60-80 hours. Microsofts recommendation is 140-160 hours. 

## Where do I start

### Study Guide
:::tip 
https://aka.ms/examcode-studyguide (for example: https://aka.ms/az305-studyguide)
:::

The Study Guide is the only document that actually outlines what is on the exam. It is your source of truth for what is on the exam. Always reference it so you stay focused on relevant exam topics and avoid getting sidetracked.
