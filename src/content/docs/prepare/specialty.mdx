:::note
Best for professionals specializing in a specific Microsoft product area, with a focus on deep product-specific knowledge and how that product fits into the cloud ecosystem.
:::

:::caution
Specialty exams are role-based and test practical skills, not just course memorization. They can be very difficult without real hands-on experience with the products.
:::

## Study Time

Average study time for specialty exams is 60-80 hours. Microsofts recommendation is 140-160 hours. 
## Where do I start

### Study Guide
:::tip 
https://aka.ms/examcode-studyguide (for example: https://aka.ms/az140-studyguide)
:::

The Study Guide is the only document that actually outlines what is on the exam. It is your source of truth for what is on the exam. Always reference it so you stay focused on relevant exam topics and avoid getting sidetracked.
