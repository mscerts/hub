## Other Ways to Support

- **Share the Hub** — Tell your colleagues, study groups, or social network about the site.
- **Contribute** — Help improve content, fix typos, or add new resources. See the [contributing guide](/contributing/).
- **Report issues** — Found a broken link or outdated info? Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/mscerts/hub/issues).
- **Join the community** — Ask questions, help others, and stay updated on [Discord](https://discord.gg/microsoft-certification-study-group-676990910176821270).

Every bit of support, whether financial or not, helps us keep the Hub alive and growing. Thank you!
