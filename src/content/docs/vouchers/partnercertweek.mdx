Earn 100% exam voucher for any Microsoft Certification Exam. 
> **Only available to Microsoft Partner Employees.** 
:::

:::danger
There are no currently no active dates for this offer. Please check back later.
:::

<Steps>
  1. Find the Partner ID of your organisation [here](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/account/v3/organization/identity). If you don't have access, you will need to contact someone who does to tell you the ID. 
  2. Register using your company email for a training you will be able to attend.
  3. Attend all days of the training.
  4. Wait for the voucher to arrive.
</Steps>
