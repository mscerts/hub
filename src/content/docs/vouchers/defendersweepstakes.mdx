## How to enter

<Steps>

1. Register for and complete the [Microsoft Defender Skilling Challenge](https://learn.microsoft.com/challenges/6381s7tkn541nz?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290) before August 21, 2026.
2. Submit an entry via the [sweepstakes entry form](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=v4j5cvGGr0GRqy180BHbR-BLGqyJXPlNlWysrop3YH1UQ1FCWDdNOUhOSFgwTE80UUpCUzFUQ1hRUi4u) before the sweepstakes period ends on August 21, 2026.
3. Winners are selected from the Prize Period: August 24, 2026 at 12:00 AM UTC – August 31, 2026 at 11:59 PM UTC. If you're selected as one of the 500 winners, you'll be contacted by Pearson VUE via email with your 50% discount voucher code, along with the steps on how to redeem it.

</Steps>
