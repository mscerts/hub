:::note
Students can get discounted Microsoft exam pricing: **30% off Fundamentals** and **45% off role-based exams**. 
:::

:::caution
45% Student discount is not available in India and China. Fundamentals student discount remains available to all students.
:::
This is a profile-based discount and can be combined with many voucher offers for even lower exam prices.

## How to claim the student discount

<Steps>

1. Go to your [Learn Profile interests](https://learn.microsoft.com/users/me/settings/interests?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290)
2. On the first page select at least **Career Paths** option, rest is up to you.
3. On the step **Careers** select **Student** and finish up the survey.
4. Schedule your exam while signed in with the same account.
5. The student discount will be applied automatically at checkout for eligible exams.
</Steps>

:::note
It will not show you that there is a discount applied, the base price of the exam will be discounted. If you are not sure if your price is discounted or not, refer to the [official price list](https://view.officeapps.live.com/op/view.aspx?src=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.pearsonvue.com%2Fcontent%2Fdam%2FVUE%2Fvue%2Fen%2Fvouchers%2Fmicrosoft-pricelist-2024.xlsx&wdOrigin=BROWSELINK)
:::
