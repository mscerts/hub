## How to claim the vouchers

<Steps>

1. Join the [Fabric Community](https://community.fabric.microsoft.com/?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290).
2. Join the [SQL Partner Community Teams channel](https://aka.ms/JoinSQLPartnerCommunity).
3. Fill out the [campaign form](https://community.fabric.microsoft.com/t5/custom/page/page-id/campaign_form?campaignID=Y2FtcGFpZ24tMTc3ODcwMjA1Njk4Mw==&WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290).
4. If you qualify for a voucher, vouchers will be distributed via a private message in the Fabric Community.
    - **Vouchers are not sent via email.** Vouchers will be sent approximately every 2 weeks.
    - There may be a longer delay between May 21st and June 9th.

</Steps>
