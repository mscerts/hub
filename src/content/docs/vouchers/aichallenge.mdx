:::note
Have a chance to win 50% off voucher for AB-720, AB-731 and AB-900 if you pass an applied skills. 
:::
:::caution
Only 5000 vouchers are available, sweepstakes end on March 2, 2026.
:::
<Steps>
1. Enter one of the 6 applied skills challenges listed [here](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/microsoft-credentials-ai-challenge?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290).

2. Pass one of the 3 Applied Skills assessments:
   - [Microsoft Applied Skills: Streamline business workflows with AI chat](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/applied-skills/streamline-business-workflows-with-ai-chat?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290)
   - [Microsoft Applied Skills: Generate reports with AI research agents](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/applied-skills/generate-reports-with-ai-research-agents?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290)
   - [Microsoft Applied Skills: Create agents in Microsoft Copilot Studio](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/applied-skills/create-agents-in-microsoft-copilot-studio?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290)

3. Enter the [sweepstakes](https://aka.ms/AIChallengeEntry)
</Steps>
