## How to claim the 80% discount

<Steps>

1. Register for [Exam AI-500 (beta)](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/certifications/multi-agent-ai-solutions-expert/?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290).
2. Schedule to take the exam on or before **August 5, 2026**.
3. When prompted for payment, use the code **AI500Wabash** to receive the 80% discount.

</Steps>
