:::note
Enter and complete **one of the nine Microsoft Ignite challenges**, then submit a sweepstakes entry form by **December 15, 2025 at 11:59 PM UTC (23:59)**, for **a chance** to receive a **50% discount Microsoft Certification voucher**.
:::

- The discount certification exam voucher is eligible for any Microsoft Certification exam conducted by Pearson Vue.
- The discount certification exam voucher is not eligible for any GitHub certification exams.

<Steps>
  1. Review the [sweepstakes terms and conditions](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/support/microsoft-ignite-challenge-official-rules#sweepstakes-terms-and-conditions).
  2. Register for and **complete one** of the below nine Microsoft Ignite challenges prior to December 15, 2025 (regardless of individual challenge end date).  <br></br><br></br>  [Create Agentic AI Solutions with Azure AI Foundry](https://aka.ms/IgniteAIAgentsChallenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage)<br></br> [Build Scalable AI Apps on Azure Database for PostgreSQL](https://aka.ms/IgniteAIAppsChallenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage) <br></br> [Fabric Data Engineer (DP-700) Ignite & Fabric Data Days Skills Challenge](https://aka.ms/Ignite-FabricDataEngineerChallenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage) <br></br> [Fabric Analytics Engineer (DP-600) Ignite & Fabric Data Days Skills Challenge](https://aka.ms/Ignite-FabricDataAnalyticsChallenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage) <br></br> [Azure Data Fundamentals | Ignite Skills Challenge](https://aka.ms/Ignite-DataFundamentalsChallenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage) <br></br> [Secure and govern AI](https://aka.ms/IgniteSecureGovernAIChallenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage) <br></br> [Code Smarter and Faster with GitHub Copilot](https://aka.ms/Ignite-GHU25Challenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage) <br></br> [Azure Migration Mastery Skills Challenge](https://aka.ms/IgniteMigrateChallenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage) <br></br> [Modernize and Innovate Skills Challenge](https://aka.ms/IgniteModernizeChallenge?wt.mc_id=ignite25_skilling_officialrulespage) <br></br>
  3. Once you complete the challenge, submit an entry via the [sweepstakes entry form](https://aka.ms/microsoftignitechallenge2025/entry) before the sweepstakes period ends on December 15, 2025.
  4. If you’re selected as a winner of one of the 50% discount certification exam vouchers (5,000 available), you will be contacted by Pearson Vue via email with your voucher code, along with the steps on how to redeem. Those steps can also be found the [‘How to redeem your prize’](https://aka.ms/microsoftignitechallenge2025/howtoredeem) page.
</Steps>
