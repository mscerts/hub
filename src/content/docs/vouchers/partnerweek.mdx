:::note
Earn 100% exam voucher for any Microsoft Certification Exam.
:::
:::danger
Microsoft has a history of retroactively changing terms and conditions of their voucher offers. Everyone is encouraged to complete this opportunity as soon as possible and redeem their voucher as soon as possible to avoid potentially losing your voucher due to Microsoft changing their mind. 
:::
:::caution
Only available to Microsoft Partner Employees. 

After attending the certification week, you need to complete a valid [LevelUp certification prep course](https://skillupwithlevelup.com/certification-prep-on-demand) and score 80% or higher on the assessment to receive your voucher. [Terms and conditions apply.](https://skillupwithlevelup.com/cert-faqs)

Keep in mind that according to the terms and conditions, all vouchers have been exhausted before the event has even started so I am not sure what exactly is the situation here. 
:::
