## How to claim the vouchers

<Steps>

1. Join the [Fabric Community](https://community.fabric.microsoft.com/plugins/common/feature/oauth2sso/sso_login_redirect?post_logout_redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fcommunity.fabric.microsoft.com%2F%3FWT.mc_id%3Dstudentamb_165290&WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290) if you have not already.
2. Watch **two (2)** Data Days sessions, [live or on-demand](https://aka.ms/dd/schedule?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290).
3. Complete **three (3)** Microsoft Learn modules from this [collection](https://aka.ms/dd/modules?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290).
4. Agree to these [terms and conditions](https://community.fabric.microsoft.com/t5/custom/page/page-id/campaign_form?campaignID=Y2FtcGFpZ24tMTc4MTEyMjc2OTc0NA==&WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290).
5. Submit your [request form](https://community.fabric.microsoft.com/t5/custom/page/page-id/campaign_form?campaignID=Y2FtcGFpZ24tMTc4MTEwNTMxMDAxMQ==&WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290) by **August 10, 2026**.
6. Check your **Fabric Community private messages** (not email) after **two (2) weeks** to see whether you are eligible.
</Steps>
