## How to enter

<Steps>

1. **Earn an eligible Applied Skill** - Choose and complete one of the following between June 15 and July 15, 2026:
   - [Enhance agents with autonomous capabilities](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/applied-skills/enhance-agents-with-autonomous-capabilities/?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290) - For Solution Architects, App Makers, and Developers building production-ready agents
   - [Generate reports with AI research agents](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/applied-skills/generate-reports-with-ai-research-agents/?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290) - For IT Professionals and Business Professionals
   - [Streamline business workflows with AI chat](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/applied-skills/streamline-business-workflows-with-ai-chat/?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290) - For Business Professionals
   - [Create and manage automated processes by using Power Automate](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/applied-skills/create-and-manage-automated-processes-with-power-automate/?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290) - For Power Platform Makers, Citizen Developers, and Business Professionals
   - [Create and manage canvas apps with Power Apps](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/applied-skills/create-manage-canvas-apps-power-apps/?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290) - For App Makers, Citizen Developers, and Business Professionals
2. **Submit your entry** - After completing the Applied Skill, submit the [official entry form](https://aka.ms/AIEntryForm) with your contact details and credential link.
3. **Winners are selected** - The drawing will happen on July 16th, if selected, you'll receive an email from **MindHub Pro by Pearson VUE** within 7 days of the drawing with your 100% off voucher code and instructions for redeeming it on an eligible Microsoft Certification exam.
4. **Redeem your voucher** - You will have until November 30th to redeem your voucher and take the exam.

</Steps>
