:::note
Earn an eligible Microsoft Applied Skills credential, submit the sweepstakes entry form, and you'll be entered for a chance to receive a 100% off Microsoft Certification exam voucher. Only 900 vouchers are available.
:::

:::caution
Entry period: June 15, 2026 at 12:00 a.m. PT – July 15, 2026 at 11:59 p.m. PT. Open to eligible participants 18 and older. Not available in Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Sudan, Syria, the Region of Crimea, Russia, and where prohibited.
:::

## How to enter

<Steps>

1. **Earn an eligible Applied Skill** - Choose and complete one of the following between June 15 and July 15, 2026:
   - [Enhance agents with autonomous capabilities](https://aka.ms/MAS-EnhanceAgent) - For Solution Architects, App Makers, and Developers building production-ready agents
   - [Create agents in Microsoft Copilot Studio](https://aka.ms/MAS-Createagents) - For App Makers, Developers, and Solution Builders
   - [Generate reports with AI research agents](https://aka.ms/MAS-ResearchAgents) - For IT Professionals and Business Professionals
   - [Streamline business workflows with AI chat](https://aka.ms/MAS-AIchat) - For Business Professionals
   - [Create and manage automated processes by using Power Automate](https://aka.ms/MAS-CreatewithPowerAutomate) - For Power Platform Makers, Citizen Developers, and Business Professionals
   - [Create and manage canvas apps with Power Apps](https://aka.ms/MAS-CanvasAppswithPowerApps) - For App Makers, Citizen Developers, and Business Professionals
2. **Submit your entry** - After completing the Applied Skill, submit the [official entry form](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/microsoft-ai-agent-skills-sweepstakes#submit-your-entry) with your contact details and credential link.
3. **Winners are selected** - If selected, you'll receive an email from **MindHub Pro by Pearson VUE** with your 100% off voucher code and instructions for redeeming it on an eligible Microsoft Certification exam.

</Steps>

## Eligible exams

Your 100% off voucher (900 total available) can be applied to one of the following Microsoft Certification exams:
