Microsoft Virtual Training Days vouchers have returned! But this time the voucher is only **50% off** unfortunately. 

## How does it work?

<Steps>

1. Register for the event
2. Attend all the sessions
3. 5-10 days after the event finishes, you will receive the voucher (See here how to redeem it [How to schedule an Exam and redeem vouchers](/guide/schedulingexam/#checkout-and-redeeming-voucher-codes). It is voucherless discount so it is important the email address is added to your Microsoft Learn account.) 
4. You will have 90 days after the event concludes to schedule the exam. Usually you can take the exam later than 90 days but it is at your own risk and support won't help you if something goes wrong, like the exam crashing.
5. Good for selected exams
6. For students, with the [student exam discount](https://learn.microsoft.com/credentials/certifications/student-discounts?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290), this adds up to **65%** discount

</Steps>

:::
