## How to claim the vouchers

<Steps>
1. Register in [AI Skills Navigator](https://aka.ms/AISF-AnnouncementBlog).
2. Complete [one unmodified, eligible Microsoft AI Skills Fest skilling playlist in its entirety on AI Skills Navigator](https://aka.ms/aiskillsfestmap_content). {/* URL is a placeholder for now */}
3. Complete [the form](https://aka.ms/aiskillsfestmap_content). {/* URL is a placeholder for now */} to request your voucher by 12:00AM UTC June 12, 2026.
4. Redeem the voucher before 12:00AM UTC August 11, 2026.
5. Take your exam before 12:00AM UTC August 18, 2026.
</Steps>

:::caution
It is highly recommended to schedule your exam as soon as you receive your voucher, with how many vouchers Microsoft is giving away, there is only so much capacity for exam deliveries so make sure to secure your spot and don't miss out on this opportunity.
:::
