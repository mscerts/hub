## How to claim the vouchers

<Steps>
1. Register in [AI Skills Navigator](https://aiskillsnavigator.microsoft.com/events/AISF2026).
2. Complete [one unmodified, eligible Microsoft AI Skills Fest skilling playlist in its entirety on AI Skills Navigator](https://aiskillsnavigator.microsoft.com/events/AISF2026).
3. Complete [the form](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=v4j5cvGGr0GRqy180BHbR8OAK2RFELFGiD-P1Ucl9TVURTI4RjNZVEs5Sk42VU5UNTJRVERBVkpPQy4u&route=shorturl). to request your voucher by 12:00AM UTC June 12, 2026.
4. Redeem the voucher before 12:00AM UTC August 18, 2026.
5. Take your exam before 12:00AM UTC October 18, 2026.
</Steps>

:::tip
AI Skill Navigator has some very invasive data collection settings for them to let you watch the videos on the platform, they have an [FAQ](https://aiskillsnavigator.microsoft.com/faq) with troubleshooting steps, I didn't manage to get it to work even after allowing them to collect all the information. Video playback works on my mobile devices. 
:::

:::caution
It is highly recommended to schedule your exam as soon as you receive your voucher, with how many vouchers Microsoft is giving away, there is only so much capacity for exam deliveries so make sure to secure your spot and don't miss out on this opportunity.
:::
