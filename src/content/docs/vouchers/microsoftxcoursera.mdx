:::note
Have a chance to earn 50% exam voucher for exam relevant to the enrolled specialization/professional certificate program.
:::

<Steps>
  1. Enroll to one of the listed courses/specialization/professional certificate programs below.
  2. Complete the enrolled course/specialization/professional certificate and earn the Coursera certificate.
  3. Check the "Messages" menu in the left sidebar inside the program you have completed. You will also be notified through an email if you have not received the voucher immediately after completing the program.
</Steps>
