:::note
Earn 100% exam voucher for DP-600 or DP-700. 
:::

:::danger
Not available for people who have received voucher from Fabric Data Days
:::

:::caution
Limited quntity of vouchers available, submission ends on February 28, 2026 or until supplies last.
:::

<Steps>
  1. Join the [Fabric Community](https://community.fabric.microsoft.com/t5/custom/page/page-id/campaign_form?campaignID=Q0FNUEFJR05fMTc2ODUyNjYwMzYzOQ==) if you haven't already
  2. Complete 3 [Microsoft Learn Fabric modules](https://learn.microsoft.com/training/browse/?products=fabric&WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290)
  3. Register for a [live session](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/reactor/series/S-1622?WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290)
  4. Be prepared to take your exam now
  5. Commit to taking your exam within 1 month of receiving your voucher
  6. Agree to these [terms and conditions](https://community.fabric.microsoft.com/t5/custom/page/page-id/campaign_form?campaignID=Q0FNUEFJR05fMTc2ODUyNzg2ODkwMw%3D%3D&WT.mc_id=studentamb_165290).
  7. Receive your voucher via a private message in the Fabric Community if you are selected.
</Steps>
