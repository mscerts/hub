## How to claim the vouchers

<Steps>

1. Register for [SkillUp with LevelUp](https://skillupwithlevelup.com/register).
2. Register for [a valid course](https://skillupwithlevelup.com/certification-prep-on-demand?voucher_offer=yes#cert-course-catalog).
